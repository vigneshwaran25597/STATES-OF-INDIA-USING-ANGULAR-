Delhi,city and and national capital territory,north-central India.The city of Delhi actually consists of two components:Ols Delhi.in the north,The historic city;and New Delhi,in the south ,since 1947 the capital of India,built in the first part of the 20th century as the capital of British India.

Sufi musi has a magic of its own,but when heard in a setup sucha as this,the music seems spectacularly amazing.There is much more to this dargah,a Muslim place of worship, than that can be detailed.But among some of the extraordinary features of the place are the Sufi songs,the devotional ambience and the commendable architecture.

RAJGHAT:

The tomb of the father of the nation,the beloved Mahatma Gandhi lays among the immaculately maintained gardens at the Rajghat.Another one of the thing that Delhi is famous for,the Rajghat has held ots charm and splendour to date.The nearby locations house two museums that have been constructed tn the memory of and dedicated to Gandhiji.

RED FORT

The Red Fort,what can be said about this carcass of sandstone that still stands tall within the city limits except that this it is amazing.The fort is now only a reminder of the last phase of the Mughal period and the infiltration of the British in the country but the significance of this place is still the same.This fort still prides itself on being the place where the national flag is hoisted each year on the Independence Day.
