MIZORAM

Mizoram literally means Land of the Hill People.Nestled along the mountains that run through the state,Mizoram is known for its pristine hills and caves. Culturally,this region is liberated from cast and gender bars.

HERE ARE SOME OF THE THINGS THAT MIZORAM IS FAMOUS FOR:

  1. LUNGLEI
  2. MAMIT
  3. KOLASIB
  4. REIEK

LUNGLEI:

This place is far away from the hustle-bustle of the city.This is one of the most breathtaking Mizoram destinatons that deserve your attention and time.its lovely landscapes and scenic setting makes it one of the top places to see in mizoram.There is also a rock-like bridge structure in the village and thus,the name Lunglei is given to it.

MAMIT:

Mamit is one of the top tourist attractions in this place and most of the wildlife enthusiasts love to go to mamit.The Dampa Wildlife Sanctuaryis one of the best attractions to visit here and you can spot peacock,malaber pied hornbill,crested serpent eagleand emerald-dove.

KOLASIB:

This is one of the best places to experience life away from the city.The Crystal clear waters of River Tlawng and the Tamdil Lake would give an experience to remember for a lifetime.The Lush green hills at the backdrop and the crystal clear water from these two water bodies make it a picture-perfect destination.

REIEK

Experience the lovely village life basking in the sun amidst the lush green fields of Reiek.For an offbeat experience in Mizoram,one must visit this village to enjoy to the fullest.If you are a traveller and want to feel every single second of your life then,this is the place youm must live at for a few weeks.A walk in this village would leave you rejuvenated totally.
