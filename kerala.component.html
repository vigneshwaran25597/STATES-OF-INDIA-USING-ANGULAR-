KERALA

Kerala is famous especially for its ecotourism initiatives and beautiful backwaters.Its unique culture and traditions,coupled with its varies demography,have made kerala one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world.

HERE ARE SOME OF THE THINGS THAT KERALA IS FAMOUS FOR:

  1. THRISSUR POORAM
  2. ECO TOURISM
  3. SCENIC BEAUTY
  4. HOUSEBOATS
  5. KATHAKALI

THRISSUR POORAM:

Called the 'mother of all temple festivals', do not miss this spectacular temple(and elephant) festival in may this year.The highlights is elephant pageantry and procession.

ECO TOURISM:

The tourism industry of Kerala is its USP and beacuse of its ecotourism initiatives, it is highly popular among foreign tourists too.

SCENIC BEAUTY:

Mention Kerala and a picture of hills and valleys with gushing waterfalls,playful streams and serene backwaters come to the mind.Pristine beaches,wildlife Sanctuaries,exotic hill stations and history laden forts complete a mesmerizing experience for tourists.

HOUSEBOATS:

Kerala is synonymous with houseboats,Which dot the framed backwaters of the state.Take a ride on these experience the sights of kerala is a new way.

KATHAKALI:

A must-see for any traveller,this intricate and beautiful dance form,the most famous to come out from this state,along with vibrant makeup is a treat for the eyes.
