GUJARAT

Gujarat is mainly famous for its Asiatic Lions,the white desert of rann of Kutch,colorful handicrafts,festivals,food,unique culture,and its many religious sites.

HERE ARE SOME OF THE THINGS THAT GUJARAT IS FAMOUS FOR:

  1. GIR
  2. SOMNATH TEMPLE
  3. RANN OF KUTCH
  4. SAPUTARA

GIR:

The only natural habitat of the Asiatic Lions,Gir National Park is the best place to come face to face with them.Truly one of the best places to visit in Gujarat,Gir is also home to a variety of other rare wild animals such as hyenas,fish owls,black bucks etc,.Spread across nearly 1412 sq km of land,this is also the only national park in India that has a community living inside its parameters,and that too in complete harmony with each other.

SOMNATH TEMPLE:

It is a famous temple in Gujarat.One of the most sacred of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva,somnath temple is not just a religious site.Lying as it is on the shores of the rough Arabian Sea,the temple is also a thing of wonder.It has been destroyed and rebuilt countless times,once by Lord soma in gold,once by Ravana in silver,once by Krishna in wood and once by King Bhimdev in stone.

RANN OF KUTCH:

Rann of Kutch is probably the most beautiful thing you will ever lay eyes on.Nested comfortably between the endless Thar Desert and the almighty Arabian Sea ,rann of kutch is a mesmerizing wonder of sand and salt.On full moon nights,the desert shines bright as a diamond,and brings a surreal sense of calm withit.

SAPUTARA:

The only "hill-station in Gujarat,Sapatura comes as a breath of fresh air in the otherwise hot and humid climate of the state.Sprawled beautifully on a densely forested plateau,Sapatura is a hot new tourist destination,with a gigantic lake surrounded by resorts.Attractions in sapatura include a boat club,an artisan village, a tribal museum,cable car and a couple of others.
