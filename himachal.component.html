HIMACHAL PRADESH

Himachal pradesh is synonymous with scenic beauty,serene landscape,and adventure sports.Literally.Himachal means "the land of snow".Situated in the western Himalaya, Himachal Pradesh has several hill stations which ae the most appropriate destination to find respite from the scorching summer heat.Now known as a destination for "all Seasons and all reasons."

HERE ARE SOME OF THE THINGS THAT HIMACHAL PRADESH IS FAMOUS FOR:

  1. KULLU-MANALI
  2. SHIMLA
  3. DHARMASHALA
  4. KASAULI
  5. BIR BILLING

KULLU-MANALI:

One of the best places to visit in Himachal Pradesh is Kullu and Manali.If you are on a lookout for fresh air and greeen mountains amidst the snow covered ranges then Kullu Manali indeed should be on the top of your list.There are numerous awesome places to visit in Manali that makes your vacation a perfect one.

SHIMLA:

One of the most famous hill stations Of Northern India-Shimla which is the right mix of Indian glory with British sophistication.It is consecrated stunning landscape and fresh climate all through the year.

DHARMASHALA:

Dharmashala definitely has to be on your list of best Himachal Pradesh destinations.Famous not only because of its climate but also you get to see how multicultural Indian community lives in harmony with the Indo Tibetan Community.

KASAULI:

Kasauli is a perfect gateway from the hustle bustle of the city and os one of the most serene places to visit in Himachal in June.Almost on the outskirts of Punjab and Haryana ,it is well connected by roadways.

BIR BILLING:

If you are an adventure seeker,Bir Blling is the mecca for adventure sports and it has to be on your list of the top Himachal Pradesh tourist places,Bir is a small village which is located on the western side of Himachal Pradesh and is blessed with some refreshing climate.
